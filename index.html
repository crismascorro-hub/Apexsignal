The Three Pillars

Indicators That Matter

Three battle-tested technical indicators, synthesized by AI into one decisive signal.

01
SMA 200
The 200-day Simple Moving Average is the gold standard trend filter. Price above SMA 200 signals a bull market structure. Below it — bearish. The agent uses this as the foundation of every analysis.
02
MACD
Moving Average Convergence Divergence tracks momentum shifts. When the MACD line crosses above the signal line, bulls take control. A widening histogram confirms the move. The agent reads the crossover and histogram for conviction.
03
RSI
The Relative Strength Index (14-period) measures overbought and oversold conditions. Above 70 signals exhaustion. Below 30 signals a potential reversal. The agent uses RSI to validate entries and warn against chasing extended moves.
