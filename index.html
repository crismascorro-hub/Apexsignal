Methodology

Three Indicators. One Answer.

Each indicator plays a distinct role. Together they form a complete picture of trend, momentum, and exhaustion.

01
SMA 200 — Trend Filter
The 200-day Simple Moving Average separates bull markets from bear markets. Price above the SMA 200 = uptrend. Price below = downtrend. It's the most widely-watched level by institutional traders.
▲ Above = Bullish
02
MACD — Momentum Signal
Moving Average Convergence Divergence reveals momentum shifts. When the MACD line crosses above the signal line with a positive histogram, momentum is building. A cross below signals weakening.
▼ Cross below = Bearish
03
RSI — Exhaustion Detector
The Relative Strength Index (14-period) measures price velocity. RSI below 30 signals oversold conditions — a potential bounce. RSI above 70 signals overbought — a potential pullback.
● 30–70 = Neutral Zone
